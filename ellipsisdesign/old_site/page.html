R. Bruce Page:
Originally from Indianapolis, Indiana. Bruce is a graduate of Indiana University's Herron School of Art. Bruces primary focus is on web and video design.

Soon after graduating with his B.F.A. in Visual Communication, Bruce went to work as an Art Director for a Bethesda, Maryland-based advertising agency. He later moved back to Indiana to be with family and began teaching for Herron.

Recently Bruce returned to Maryland and obtained a position as a full-time graphic design instructor with Hagerstown Business College. He and fellow instructor Tom Finley realized they had a common goal... Ellipsis Design.
