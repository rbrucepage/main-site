An artist since childhood, Dawn Walker has made her life a quest for beauty - in people, in her community, in the larger world and earth around us. Her pastel paintings glow with color while providing tranquility and peace of mind.  "We're so busy in the world," she explains. "No one has time to stop and really look at God’s creations.  I paint what God put here." 

When she discovered the lavender fields of France, Dawn knew she had come to her spiritual home.
