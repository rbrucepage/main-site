"Fields of lavender as far as the eye can see, green and purple acres surrounded by the vibrant gold of sunflowers. In the distance, soft, gray peaks meet a cloudless, blue sky. Am I in heaven? I brush my hands over the tops of tiny flowers as the fragrance and butterflies dance in the breeze. I am not in heaven… but in another of God’s dreams as His spirit fills me with love. It is in these moments that I know my dreams come true."

-- Dawn Walker
