Leaving us a message!		 Ellipsis Design was established in late 2002. Our two principal partners,
R. Bruce Page and Tom Finley, both enjoy sharing their knowledge and passion for design by teaching courses for the Hagerstown Business College located in Hagerstown, MD. While primarily focusing on web/multi-media based projects, we are experienced with, and regularly create print advertising and marketing materials as well...
 
 
 

...Ellipsis Design understands the importance of staying current within the design industry, both creatively and technologically. We also work hard to keep up with the ever-changing and fast-paced world of business. We are constantly researching design within various industries so as to keep our creative skills fresh and cutting edge.

Everything communicates a concept on some level. Even the absence of a given element can convey a message. It is our task to achieve a successful balance between the visual and the cerebral, regardless of the audience. Clear communication means no corners can be cut and no questions should remain unanswered.
